My Hobbies

Dancing

I've been training in Indian Classical Dance for the past 15 years, and am super blessed to be on UC Berkeley's Premier Competitive Indian Classical Dance Team, Natya at Berkeley! Check out some of our best competitive pieces and dance videos here!

Eating

I LOVE to eat! From unhealthy favorites such as pizza, boba tea, mozzerella sticks, and chocolate cake, to healthier options like CPK's Santa Fe Power Bowl, tomato soup, or a burrata cheese plate, food truly is a major source of joy in my life and my favorite thing to bond over!

Traveling + Photography

I love to travel around the world and around the United States with friends and family! Hopefully one day I can visit all seven continents and all seven wonders of the world. I also love to take photos of the places I visit, so here are some of the most recent places I've traveled to and some of my favorite pictures from them!

SINGAPORE
What a beautiful country, filled with landmarks such as the famous Merlion in Sentosa Island, and also rich culture like this beautiful Hindu Temple pictured!
FUN FACT: I speak Tamil, one of the national languages of Singapore.
CANADA
Another country filled with gorgeous images of nature, from waterfalls, to the flowers at the famous Butchart Gardens. Spent our whole week long vacation just relaxing, enjoying the scenery around me, and of course eating some great food!
NEW YORK CITY
Took a weekend trip this summer to visit this city that never sleeps! I used to do Theatre in high school so loved watching a Broadway muscial!
DISNEYLAND
Who wouldn't love to be in the happiest place on earth?! Especially, when my aunt made my family matching shirts so we'd be easy to spot in the crowds!
YELLOWSTONE NATIONAL PARK
No wonder this place is a natural wonder of the world, it is definitely a must-see! From herds of bison to seeing Old Faithful, the park truly took my breath away!
